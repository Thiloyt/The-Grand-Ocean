    What is a hotel brunch?

    A hotel brunch is a meal that combines breakfast and lunch and is usually served as a buffet. It is a popular dining option for people who want a hearty meal that can last them until dinner. Hotel brunches are often offered on weekends and holidays and feature a variety of dishes, including hot and cold breakfast items, salads, soups, sandwiches, seafood, and desserts.

    What are the must-try dishes in a hotel brunch?

    Hotel brunches offer a wide range of dishes, but there are some that you should not miss. These include:

    • Eggs Benedict
    • Waffles and Pancakes
    • Smoked Salmon
    • Cocktail Shrimps
    • Crab Cakes
    • Assorted Pastries and Breads
    • Assorted Fruits
    • Mimosas and Bloody Marys

    Where can you find the best hotel brunch?

    There are many hotels that offer brunch, but some of the best ones are:

    1. The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong
    2. The Plaza Hotel, New York
    3. The Peninsula, Chicago
    4. The St. Regis, Bali
    5. The Landmark, London
